- 0020 space
- 2002 en space
- 2003 em space

- 00A0 no-break space
- 2004 third em space
- 2006 sixth em space
- 2007 figure space
- 2008 punctuation space
- 2009 thin space
- 200A hair space
- 202F narrow no-break space
- 205F medium math space
